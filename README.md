# Top-100-Currently-Global-Selling-Steam-Games-Market-Analysis
This project is an analysis of the top 100 currently global selling Steam games. Using Advanced SQL, I identified review score trends by publishers, categorized games by price tiers to observe which tier yields the highest review scores, analyzed overall sales growth over time, and found games in the market which are within the bottom 25 percent quartilie in terms of copies sold but has a review score over 85.

🎯 Project Goals

The main goal of this project was to explore the following questions:
- Are the most sold games from publishers generally the highest rated games?
- Does a higher price generally mean a better quality game?
- Which year did we see the most growth in sales and revenue?
- Which high quality games are flying under the radar in terms of copies sold?

🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
- SQL(MYSQL): Window Functions (RANK, PERCENT_RANK), CTEs, and Data Cleaning
- Price Analysis: Segmenting market data into price tiers (Free, Budget, Mid-Tier, Premium) to analyze sale and review score trends
- Translating raw database queries into actionable market insights

💡 Key Insights
- Large scale publishers maintain high sales even when quality-to-price ratios are skewed.
- Budget tier games consistently yield highest average review scores.
- The year 2012 is identified as the "Golden Year" for sales.
- RimWorld is a top tier performer in terms of high reviews but lacks in overall copies sold.
